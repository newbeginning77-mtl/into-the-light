Dawn — About

I'm Kangeun — an educator, storyteller, and builder of bridges through sport.

My work lives where learning, integrity, and human connection meet. I design global education programs in international sport, lead teams across cultures and continents, and believe deeply that sport can be a language of peace.

I'm a Korean expat in Montréal, a mother, a teacher in my faith community, and a photographer who chases light the way this page does — from darkness toward dawn. The name of this place comes from a promise: that we are called out of darkness, into marvelous light.

This is the journey. Thank you for walking it with me.
